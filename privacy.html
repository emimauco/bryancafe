PRIVACY POLICY

At Bryan's Café, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

Information We Collect:

When you fill out the inquiry form on our website, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Any additional information you provide in the message field

How We Use Your Information:

The information collected through the inquiry form is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to your inquiries or requests
  • To improve our services and user experience
  • To communicate with you regarding updates or promotions, if you have opted in

Information Sharing:

We do not share, sell, or trade your personal information with third parties, except in cases where we are legally required to do so.

Security:

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@brianscafe.com.
